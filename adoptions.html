Adoptions

Adoption process

We're open Saturdays from 10 AM to 2 PM at Petco Sunnyvale – just drop by! To visit at another time, please submit an application or email adopt@bayareacats.org. We believe in a simple, straightforward adoption process and allow same-day adoptions.


Adoption application

Available cats & kittens

Visit us at Bay Area Cats' Adoption Center inside Petco at 160 E El Camino Real, Sunnyvale 94087.

Adoption fees:

  • Kittens: $150 ($250 for a pair)
  • Adults: $100

All cats are spayed/neutered, vaccinated, microchipped, dewormed, and flea-treated.
